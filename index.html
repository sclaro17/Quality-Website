Our Story

Nearly Five Decades of Craft

Quality Sign & Marquee began in 1974 as Southland Display Co. — a family trade built on precision fabrication and a commitment to showing up ready. In 2007 we became Quality Sign & Marquee, and through two brand evolutions and five decades, the core hasn't changed: we build the first thing audiences see.

From a single circuit installation to a full convention floor in Las Vegas, we handle every phase in-house. Our 50,000 sq ft facility houses a climate-controlled print room, woodshop, metal shop, and packaging and assembly — no subcontracting, no surprises.

Studios of every size trust QSM because the work is consistent, the team is experienced, and the standards haven't drifted in nearly 50 years.

Family-owned since 1974 — consistent leadership, consistent quality.
Serving major, mini-major, and independent studios nationwide.
Competitive, adaptable pricing for studios of all sizes — scale up or down.

1974

Founded as Southland Display Co. in Covina, CA — fabrication, signage, and theatrical displays for local exhibitors.

1980s–90s

Expanded circuit-wide relationships with major theatrical chains. Developed marquee manufacturing as a core specialty.

2000s

Grew convention services nationally. First CinemaCon and ShowEast programs. Began expanding fulfillment capabilities.

2007

Rebranded as Quality Sign & Marquee. Launched Quality Fulfillment Services as the dedicated fulfillment arm.

2010s

Expanded to 50,000 sq ft. Added international convention work — CineEurope and global studio programs.

Today

Fully solar-powered facility. All-electric lift fleet. Five service pillars serving studios of every size — nationwide, under one roof.

Our Commitment

Quality Meets Responsibility

Commitment to our clients and commitment to doing the work responsibly go together. Here's what that looks like at QSM.

☀️

Solar-Powered Facility

Our entire 50,000 sq ft warehouse and production facility runs on a 100%-owned solar power system — clean energy without a utility dependency.

♻️

Responsible Waste Management

Paper, cardboard, and plastics are recycled locally. We work with community partners to keep production waste out of landfill where possible.

⚡

All lift equipment — including forklifts — is electric-only. Zero emissions on the warehouse floor, day in and day out.
